Hello! I am a PhD Student in Paleoclimatology at the University of Bristol, where I focus on the individual impact of insolation, greenhouse gases and ice sheet changes on the climate variations over the past 3.6 Ma. My research combines climate modelling work and model-proxy comparison. 

I'm passionate about climate variability over broad range of timescales, and I enjoy communicating science to diverse audiences. I am also curious to discover interdisciplinary work to learn more about past human-climate interactions. When I am not working on my climate model simulations, you might find me running slowly, sailing (contemplatively), reading classic literature or learning about Art history.
